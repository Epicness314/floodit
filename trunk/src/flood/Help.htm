Flood It

The object of the game is to make the entire grid all the same color, in as few moves as possible.

When you click one of the buttons at the bottom of the screen, the square upper left corner turns to that color, as do all the squares of the same color that are adjacent to it. And all the squares of the same color that are adjacent to those squares. And so on.

Just try it, you'll get the idea. Go for those high scores!
